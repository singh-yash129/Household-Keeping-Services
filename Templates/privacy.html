Privacy Policy

At Household Helpers, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information from and about users of our website, including:

  • Personal Information: This includes your name, email address, phone number, and other details that you provide when contacting us, making inquiries, or booking services.
  • Payment Information: If you make a purchase or service booking, we collect payment details (credit card, billing address) for transaction purposes.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website, such as IP address, browser type, access time, and referring website URLs.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to enhance your user experience by remembering your preferences and analyzing site traffic.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our household services, including scheduling, billing, and communication with you.
  • Improve and personalize your experience on our website by offering relevant content and service recommendations.
  • Process your payments securely for the services you book through our platform.
  • Respond to your inquiries, provide customer support, and resolve any issues that arise.
  • Analyze site traffic and user behavior to improve our website's functionality and performance.

3. Sharing of Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party vendors who help us provide and improve our services, such as payment processors and IT support. These third parties are obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes for which we provide it.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

4. Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement a variety of security measures to protect it from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. These measures include secure data encryption, access controls, and regular security audits.

5. Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we have collected about you.
  • Correction: Request corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal information, subject to certain legal exceptions.
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of receiving promotional communications from us at any time by following the unsubscribe instructions in our emails or contacting us directly.

6. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience on our website by remembering your preferences and collecting statistical data about your interactions with our site. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect the functionality of our website.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the revised version will indicate the date it was last updated. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy regularly to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
