Frequently Asked Questions

1. What types of cleaning services do you offer?
We offer residential cleaning, deep cleaning, move-in/move-out cleaning, office cleaning, post-construction cleaning, and more.
2. How do I book a cleaning service?
You can book through our website or by calling our customer service team. Just select your service, and choose a convenient date and time.
3. Do I need to provide my own cleaning supplies?
No, our cleaners come equipped with all necessary supplies. If you prefer specific products, feel free to let us know, and we can use them.
4. Are your cleaners insured and background-checked?
Yes, all our cleaners are thoroughly background-checked and insured for your peace of mind.
5. What is included in a standard cleaning?
A standard cleaning includes dusting, vacuuming, sweeping, mopping, bathroom cleaning, and kitchen surface cleaning. Additional services can be added.
