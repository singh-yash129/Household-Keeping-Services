Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What areas do you serve?

We currently provide services in New York City, Brooklyn, Queens, Manhattan, and Staten Island. If you’re unsure if we cover your area, please contact us.

2. How do I book a service?

Booking is easy! You can call us directly, fill out our contact form on the website, or use our online booking tool to schedule an appointment at your convenience.

3. What are your hours of operation?

We operate from 8 AM to 6 PM, Monday through Saturday. If you need services outside these hours, we offer flexible scheduling by appointment.

4. What if I’m not satisfied with the service?

We aim to provide 100% satisfaction. If you’re not happy with the quality of service, please let us know within 24 hours, and we will address the issue at no additional cost.

5. Do I need to provide cleaning supplies?

No, we bring all our own cleaning supplies and equipment. However, if you have specific products you prefer us to use, let us know, and we can accommodate your request.
