Comprehensive Household Services

At Household Helpers, we understand that every household has different needs. That’s why we offer flexible services that can be customized to meet your specific requirements. Here are some of the key services we provide:

1. House Cleaning

Our professional cleaning team offers both regular cleaning and deep cleaning options. We handle everything from dusting and vacuuming to scrubbing kitchens and bathrooms. Our eco-friendly cleaning products ensure your home is both clean and safe for your family.

2. Home Repairs and Maintenance

From minor repairs to larger home improvement projects, our skilled handymen are ready to assist with electrical, plumbing, and general repairs. Whether it's fixing a leaky faucet, replacing light fixtures, or patching drywall, we provide prompt and reliable service.

3. Laundry and Ironing Services

We know laundry can be time-consuming. Let us take care of it for you! Our laundry services include washing, drying, folding, and ironing. We take great care in handling your garments to ensure they come back fresh, clean, and neatly folded.

4. Grocery Shopping & Errands

Save time by letting us handle your grocery shopping. Just provide a list, and we'll take care of the rest! We also offer other errand services such as picking up dry cleaning, collecting prescriptions, and mailing packages.

5. Gardening & Lawn Care

Maintain a beautiful outdoor space with our gardening and lawn care services. Our team will help you with regular lawn mowing, garden maintenance, trimming hedges, and planting seasonal flowers to enhance the beauty of your garden.
